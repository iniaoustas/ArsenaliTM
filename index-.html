What is the Marathon

A Tango Marathon is a multi-day social dance gathering where tango enthusiasts from different countries meet to share their passion. Unlike festivals, marathons focus on continuous dancing — usually three or four days of back-to-back milongas with international DJs, in a friendly, non-competitive atmosphere. The goal is to enjoy music, connection, and community, often in a single venue that becomes the dancers’ home for the entire event.
