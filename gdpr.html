Privacy Policy (GDPR Compliance)

Arsenali TM26 Tango Marathon respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Arsenali TM26 Organizing Team
Venue: Μεγάλο Αρσενάλι, Michail Afentoulief Street, Chania 731 32, Greece
Contact: arsenalitangomarathon@gmail.com

2. Personal Data We Collect

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (if provided)
  • Registration details (selected pass, preferences)
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers, if applicable)

3. Purpose of Processing

  • Manage your registration and participation in the event
  • Communicate event updates, schedule changes, and logistics
  • Comply with legal and tax requirements (if applicable)
  • Ensure the smooth organization of the event

4. Legal Basis

  • Consent (when you register or contact us)
  • Contractual necessity (to deliver the event you registered for)
  • Legal obligations (e.g., invoicing where relevant)

5. Data Retention

Personal data will be stored only as long as necessary to fulfill the above purposes. Registration data will normally be deleted within 12 months after the event unless required for legal reasons.

6. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to the organizing team.
  • In case of payment processing, data may be handled by secure third-party providers (e.g., bank/payment gateway).

7. Cookies & Analytics

Our website may use cookies for basic functionality and anonymous statistics. You can disable cookies in your browser settings. If analytics tools are used, they collect anonymized traffic data only.

8. Your Rights

Under GDPR you have the right to access, correct, delete your data, and withdraw consent at any time. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Data Protection Authority of Greece.

To exercise your rights, contact us at: arsenalitangomarathon@gmail.com

9. Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available on our website.
